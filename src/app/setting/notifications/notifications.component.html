Notifications

  • Social Media
    Comments
    • Notifications for comments on your posts and replies to your comments.
    Update from Friends
    • Notifications about your friends' activities, like when any friend upload or share a post or photo.
    Friend Requests
    • Notification alert when someone sends you a friend request or accepts your friend request.
    Birthdays
    • Notifications about upcoming birthdays of all your friends.
    Groups
    • Notifications about all activities including posts, likes, comments in Groups you've joined.
    • Notifications of all activities including posts, likes, comments in Groups you're managing.
    Discover Friends
    • Notifications about people you may know or friend suggestions.
  • Events
    In Groups
    • Notify when any new event is created or shared in group you manage.
    • Notify when any event is deleted in group you manage.
    • Notify when any new event is created or shared in the group you joined.
    Events you created
    • Notify when any person select interested or buy pass of event you created.
    • Notify when any person shares your created event.
  • Classified Ads
    • Notify when any person shares your created ad.
    • Notify when any person contacts you through your posted ad.
  • Jobs
    • Notify when any person shares your created ad.
  • Polling
    • Notify when any person shares your created poll.
    • Notify when admin accepts your created poll request.
    • Notify when your created poll ends.
  • Donation
    • Notify when any person shares your created campaign.
    • Notify when any person donated in campaign created by you.
  • Matrimonial
    • Notify when any person shares your profile.
  • Volunteer
    • Notify when any person shares your profile.
