Terms & Conditions

Below are the terms and conditions you should accept when registering as a volunteer.
  • You acknowledge that your information, such as contact information and email address, will be posted on the Volunteers page.
  • You acknowledge that volunteering is not a profession, and you will not be compensated for the services you provide.
  • You can contribute 4 to 6 hours per week to the community to help your fellow members in any way related to your profession, such as if you are a lawyer and can help them find a suitable forum to seek legal help.
  • You acknowledge that the interview will be conducted through a video call service and Dil Hai Pakistani reserves the right to approve or reject your volunteer request.
  • You acknowledge that you are not engaged in and convicted of any criminal activity.
